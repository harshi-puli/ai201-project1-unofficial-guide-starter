Local Events
Among Berkeley’s many incredible culinary spots, the breakfast places stand out as some of the best I’ve experienced. Whether you’re craving something sweet or savory, Berkeley’s breakfast restaurants have something for you. In this article, I’ll share my picks for the must-visit spots that make this city a breakfast lover’s paradise.
La Note
La Note offers a charming French-inspired breakfast experience. The ambiance is inviting, quaint and homely, with cozy indoor seating and a delightful outdoor patio perfect for a sunny day. From fluffy pancakes to French omelets, La Note serves up a broad range of French-style dishes that blend rich flavors with a charming atmosphere. It’s a gem for those seeking upscale, delicious breakfast food in one of the most picturesque locations.
Berkeley Social Club
A true staple in the Berkeley community, Berkeley Social Club brings a unique twist to traditional breakfast with a menu that fuses Asian flavors with American classics. Their dishes stand out for inventive ingredients and bold flavors, like their famous Millionaire’s Bacon — perfectly crispy, sweet and savory all at once. This restaurant is a local favorite, promising a breakfast experience you won’t forget.
Eggy’s Neighborhood Kitchen
Located on Northside, Eggy’s offers big portions perfect for sharing, making it a great spot for groups. Its rustic patio adds a charming touch to the casual atmosphere, where you can enjoy delicious dishes such as  chicken and waffles and egg dishes from chilaquiles to creative benedicts. It’s a relaxed, kitchen-style setting with top-notch food that never disappoints.
Baker & Commons
Baker & Commons is a charming, cafe-style diner in Elmwood, ideal for a relaxed coffee break. With a variety of options from pastries to avocado toast, it’s a cozy place to grab a quick morning bite. The laid-back atmosphere makes it a great place to unwind, and you can easily enjoy a stroll around the shops nearby afterward.
Crepevine
Crepevine is a beloved Bay Area eatery that, you can guess, has the perfect sweet and savory crepes for any mood that you’re in. A local, family-owned restaurant, Crepevine has locations across the Bay. Aside from its specialty crepes, Crepevine also offers a versatile menu with sandwiches, salads, pasta and more. Its festive, warm environment creates the perfect setting to relax and enjoy a meal with friends or family.
Whatever your ideal breakfast looks like, I can guarantee that these standout spots truly capture the city’s unique charm and welcoming vibe, making Berkeley an unforgettable destination for any breakfast enthusiast.
